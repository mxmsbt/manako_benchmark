Methodology: All three models evaluated against independently generated ground truth (200 images, 5,883 annotations). No model was involved in generating the GT annotations. SN44 is the winning person detection model from Bittensor SN44 subnet training (manak0/Detect-Person-winner, 18.8MB ONNX, 4.4M params). SAM3 is Meta's foundation model (facebook/sam3, 848M params, 3.4GB, requires 24GB+ GPU). Roboflow model: cctv-naxyo/1 (cloud API, estimated YOLOv8s, ~22MB, ~11M params). SN44 inference cost: $0 (CPU). SAM3 cost based on A100 spot pricing ($1.84/hr, ~8.5 img/sec). Roboflow cost based on published API pricing.
