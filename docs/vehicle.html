Methodology: All three models evaluated against independently generated ground truth (200 images, 1,792 annotations, 3 classes: car/bus/truck). No model was involved in generating the GT annotations. SN44 is the winning vehicle detection model from Bittensor SN44 subnet training (alfred8995/kane001, ~52 GFLOPs at 1280x1280, 4.4M params). SAM3 is Meta's foundation model (facebook/sam3, ~2,900 GFLOPs, 848M params, requires 24GB+ GPU). Roboflow model: vehicles-q0x2v/1 (cloud API, ~29 GFLOPs, ~11M params).
